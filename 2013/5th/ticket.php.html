› Ticket Pick-Up:
Pick up tickets from June 3 to June 28
› Pick up tickets at the ASB Den at Lynbrook on weekdays from 7 AM to 7 PM.
› Bring your credit card (used in the purchase) to verify your ID.
› Tickets may be picked up once they are purchased.
› You may use your email confirmation as a receipt.

Tickets may be picked up the day of graduation, but it is recommended that you pick up your tickets in advance so you are not late to the ceremony.
