spoons
One of the most cherished memories of the Class of 2013 is a game in which the goal is to target and eliminate other players by catching and tagging them with a spoon when they do not possess a specific, announced item. Commonly known as Assassin, this game is now known within the Lynbrook community as Spoons, a game in which survival is key.

"Us choir kids formed a truce so that we could survive together.
Safety in numbers!"

- Arvind P.
graduate
