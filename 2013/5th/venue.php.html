› Venue:
Lynbrook's 2013 graduation will be held at the Viking Stadium.
The box office and doors will open at 9:00 AM.

Lynbrook Stadium includes full accessibility for individuals with wheelchairs. There is no smoking at Lynbrook High School.
 › Parking:
Parking is free in the Lynbrook parking lot and on neighboring streets. Graduation is a large event, so carpooling, walking, biking, and public transport are encouraged.
