    The Purpose of this site is to help fund the Labette County High School Future Business Leaders of America students attending the National Leadership Conference (NLC).Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) is the largest career student organization in the world and focuses on leadership and career development.

    Getting these students to compete and attend these public events, such as nationals, allow for more experience and help further their education in business. To not only have people generously help donate funding. People from all over have donated lightly used popular items for resale value. Profit from items that are resold go directly to students and help funding their trip to the National Leadership Conference.
