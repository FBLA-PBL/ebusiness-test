    Event Planning 

    Stylish meetings and events.

     

    Whether you're looking to host an inspiring business meeting or a stunning event, the iconic Olympic Pavilion stands alone among Seattle events venues. The gorgeous event venues can fit nearly any size gathering, and the exceptional event staff that has a history of throwing the Pacific Northwest's best functions. 

     

    Just minutes from downtown Seattle and under an hour to the adventurous Cascade Mountains, world-class golf, and lush evergreen forests is a perfect blend of urban and outdoors proximity for your attendees.

    Weddings
    REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL >>>

    Make your vows truly everlasting at a Northwest icon—the Olympic Pavilion.

     

    A beautiful garden conveniently located at the pavilion, the Olympic Pavilion also includes two luxurious meeting halls, and a grand Olympic Ballroom to provide an excellent space for your Wedding.

     

    With dark wood highlights, deep copper, rich amber, coffee and sky-toned carpet and fabrics, the color palette is dramatic, yet firmly grounded in a Northwest feel. The clean lines, textured wall coverings and the organic patterns within the fabrics all create a classic, yet modern sense of inherent grace. The dramatic artistic lighting fixtures can be customized to match any theme or mood.

     

    Ladies and gentlemen, raise a glass to the Northwest's best wedding venue.
